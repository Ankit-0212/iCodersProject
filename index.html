ACTIVITIES

Geeky Sessions

There will be regular sessions on technologies and programming apart from our syllabus by Members of the club, Teachers, Professionals. We will give priority to Open Source Technologies.
There will be two types of Sessions :
A) For Members Only
B) For College students

Coding

There will be regular online coding competitions for members as well as for the whole college. This will help students to become good programmers. And we will also have regular practice sessions among members, online as well as offline.

Events

Our best members of the club will represent our college in Tech Events, Open Source, Hackathon, etc. We will also make teams and participate in some big Online Tech Events & Competitions like the Microsoft Imagine Cup, Google Summer of Code and etc

Projects

Our members will be involved in some Innovative Projects like creating apps for different Operating Systems, Web apps and etc. as teamwork under the guidance of our teacher's skills (Basically we will focus on 2 projects per year one in the summer and one in winter ).

HISTORY IN THE MAKING

It all started in the year 2015. That time our college used to have few clubs/societies like Karate and NCC but nothing technical. So starting a tech club initially focusing on CSE & IT students was required.Then on 15/05/2015, a Facebook page called iCoders was created.Under the supervision of HoD IT Prof. Saikat Maity (He was also in-charge of CSE that time) permission and required formalities were proceeded.

Some of the activities include:
1. Coding Competitions
2. Events
3. Projects
4. Classes
5. Workshops

All the required permission for the iCoders were granted on 22/09/2015. Now the team realised that we would need more members to operate this club.So many more people joined in this initiative. Promotion of the club in our college started and got a huge response from juniors, batchmates and seniors. By the end of 2015, we had around 200+ members registered in the club.

Till that time we didn’t have any perfect official logo of the club. So with the help of a senior member, we designed the current logo. (Trivia: The i in the logo is actually a barcode for iCoders and can be scanned. The ‘smile’ is inspired by the Amazon logo. The ‘hat’ is inspired by the white hat hacker. The o and d are designed in such a way that it looks similar to the specs of a geek.)

Afterwards iCoders got associated with Hackerearth,Internshala etc. Later we started doing events and competitions on behalf of the club in association with various organizations. We also started hosting various workshops and events for the annual college tech fest.

By 2017 we got our own office room in one of the college building with the help of our Principal and HoD CSE Prof. Chandan Koner.

Before leaving the college we handed over iCoders to our juniors who were actively involved with us and I hope that this tradition will never stop.
